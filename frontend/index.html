FAQ

Are these glasses dishwasher safe?

To preserve the intricate detailing and luxury finish of our metal stems, we highly recommend gently hand-washing all MAIRAÉ glassware with warm water and mild soap.

Do you sell glasses individually or in sets?

Both! You can purchase our statement pieces individually, or build a cohesive look with our luxury glassware sets.

Are these suitable for gifting?

Absolutely. Every MAIRAÉ order is carefully packaged, making them ideal gifts for bar lovers, anniversaries, and birthdays.
